What is Synapse?

Named after the synapses that connect our brain’s cognitive functions, Synapse protects the most important part of the body: the head.
Promoting the use of safety gear in action sports by seamlessly blending style with protection, Synapse delivers peace of mind and real-world functionality, without compromising personal expression or everyday comfort.

Addressing the issue of young skaters avoiding helmets due to social stigmas within the skating community,
Synapse hoodies fuse safety with style, offering a comfortable and confidence-enhancing alternative to traditional helmets.
