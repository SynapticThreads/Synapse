What is Synapse?

Named after the synapses that connect our brain’s cognitive functions,
Synapse protects the most important part of the body: the head.

Promoting the use of safety gear in action sports by seamlessly blending style with protection,
Synapse delivers peace of mind and real-world functionality, ~ without compromising personal expression or everyday comfort.

Addressing the issue of young skaters avoiding helmets due to social stigmas within the skating community,
Synapse hoodies fuse safety with style, offering a comfortable and confidence-enhancing alternative to traditional helmets.

Engineering & Design

Engineered to absorb and disperse energy during falls,
Synapse incorporates a lightweight, impact-resistant liner
woven directly into the hoodie’s design,
freeing skaters from the burden of carrying or wearing clunky helmets,
and making protection feel natural, unobtrusive and subtle.

The Risk Is Real — So Is the Solution

Head safety is no joke. Unlike broken arms, legs, fingers, or toes, head injuries can cause lasting cognitive impairment or even DEATH.

Synapse Hoodies are designed to give skaters a sleek, comfortable middle ground — offering protection without the bulk of traditional hardshell gear or the risk of going without.
